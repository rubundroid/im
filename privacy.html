Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Indent Maker" app and website collect, use, and protect your information. By using this service, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

1. Information Collection, Analytics, and Use

To improve our app and understand user demographics, we use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics and custom tracking via Google Forms). This process may collect your IP address to determine your general location (City/State) and the institution name you voluntarily provide. This information is strictly used for internal analytical purposes to improve user experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third-party marketing agencies.

2. Data Collection for Cloud Sync (Google Login)

To provide the "Cloud Sync" feature, our application uses Google Sign-In. When you choose to log in, we collect basic profile information provided by Google, which includes your Name, Email Address, and Profile Picture. This information is used exclusively to authenticate your account and securely sync your indent drafts.

3. Third-Party Services (Google Firebase)

We use Google Firebase as our secure backend service to manage authentication and store your synced indent data. Firebase strictly adheres to industry-standard security protocols. Your indent data is stored securely in the cloud and is accessible only by you.

4. Offline Functionality & Local Storage

If you choose to use the application without logging in to the cloud, this app uses your device's local storage (Cache and LocalStorage) to enable offline functionality and save your draft indents. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

5. Data Retention and Account Deletion

You have full control over your data. We have provided a dedicated 'Delete Account' option directly within the application. By using this feature, you can completely and permanently delete your account along with all your synced cloud data from our servers at any time. You can also delete individually saved indents from the File Manager. We do not retain any of your data once your account is deleted.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
